# Disruption Response Engine

## Scenario 1 — Supplier Delivery Failure
Trigger:
Primary supplier fails to deliver on time.

Inputs:
- SKU
- Supplier
- OTIF
- Lead time
- Alternate supplier

Output:
- Emergency reorder plan
- Alternate supplier recommendation
- Reorder quantity

---

## Scenario 2 — Critical Stockout Risk
Trigger:
Days of cover falls below 3 days.

Output:
- Immediate replenishment plan
- Stockout prevention strategy

---

## Scenario 3 — Demand Spike Above Forecast
Trigger:
Demand suddenly increases above forecast.

Output:
- Increased reorder quantity
- Faster replenishment strategy

---

## Scenario 4 — Lead Time Suddenly Increased
Trigger:
Supplier lead time increases unexpectedly.

Output:
- Safety stock adjustment
- Advanced reorder scheduling

---

## Scenario 5 — Inventory Instability
Trigger:
Inventory fluctuates frequently.

Output:
- Inventory stabilization plan
- Supply continuity actions
