1. SUPPLIER RELATIONSHIP ANALYSIS

Purpose

Provide visibility into upstream dependencies and downstream business impact.

Implementation

Added supplier_relationships field to at-risk supplier alerts.

Fields

* upstream_suppliers
* downstream_impact
* criticality
* backup_options

Example

Supplier SUP-0063

Upstream Suppliers:

* Raw Material Supplier A

Downstream Impact:

* Assembly Plant B
* Assembly Plant C

Criticality:
Yes

Backup Options:
2

Business Value

Allows executives to understand supply chain dependencies and identify critical suppliers.
